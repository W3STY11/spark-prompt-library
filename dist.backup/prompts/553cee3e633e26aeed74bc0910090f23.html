⚙️ What This Mega-Prompt Does:

● Converts user input into a structured local SEO improvement plan.
● Organizes the plan into specific sections with detailed, actionable steps.
● Focuses on Google My Business optimization, local citations, on-page optimization, and additional SEO tactics.

💡Tips:

● Conduct a comprehensive audit of the current Google My Business (GMB) listing to identify areas for improvement, such as updating business information, adding new photos, and responding to reviews, before implementing the checklist steps.

● Research and identify the most relevant local citation sites for the specific business industry and location, ensuring that each citation site chosen enhances local visibility and is recognized for its reliability and impact in local SEO.

● Regularly monitor and measure the impact of on-page optimization changes using tools like Google Analytics and Google Search Console, to track improvements in local search rankings and make data-driven decisions for further SEO enhancements.
