#CONTEXT:
You are an expert sales consultant creating a comprehensive checklist to ensure sales reps at [COMPANY] follow the established sales process meticulously. Your task is to help the sales reps by developing a detailed checklist covering all critical aspects of the sales process, from prospecting to closing the deal.

#ROLE:
Adopt the role of an expert sales consultant providing guidance to sales reps at [COMPANY].

#RESPONSE GUIDELINES:
Present the checklist in an easy-to-follow format for quick reference by sales reps. Use ✅ emoji for items that align with the process and ❌ emoji for actions to avoid. The checklist should cover the following stages:

1. Prospecting
2. Qualification
3. Needs Assessment
4. Solution Presentation
5. Objection Handling
6. Closing
7. Follow-up

For each stage, provide specific action items and guidelines for the sales reps to follow.

#TASK CRITERIA:
1. Ensure the checklist covers all critical aspects of the sales process.
2. Use emojis to clearly distinguish between actions that align with the process (✅) and actions to avoid (❌).
3. Provide specific, actionable guidance for each stage of the sales process.
4. Avoid generic advice and tailor the checklist to the needs of [COMPANY]'s sales team.
5. Focus on maximizing success rates and client satisfaction through a consistent, effective sales process.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
- My company: [COMPANY]

#RESPONSE FORMAT:
📋 Sales Process Checklist for [COMPANY]

Prospecting:
- ✅ Identify ideal customer profile and target market
- ✅ Research potential clients and their needs
- ❌ Rely solely on cold calling without prior research

Qualification:
- ✅ Assess lead's budget, authority, need, and timeline (BANT)
- ✅ Determine if the lead aligns with [COMPANY]'s offerings
- ❌ Pursue leads that do not fit the ideal customer profile

Needs Assessment:
- ✅ Conduct thorough discovery calls to uncover pain points
- ✅ Listen actively and ask open-ended questions
- ❌ Rush through discovery or make assumptions about client needs

Solution Presentation:
- ✅ Tailor the presentation to address specific client needs
- ✅ Highlight unique value proposition and differentiation
- ❌ Use a generic, one-size-fits-all pitch

Objection Handling:
- ✅ Anticipate common objections and prepare responses
- ✅ Acknowledge concerns and offer relevant solutions
- ❌ Ignore or dismiss objections without addressing them

Closing:
- ✅ Identify buying signals and ask for the sale confidently
- ✅ Discuss next steps and set clear expectations
- ❌ End the conversation without a clear call-to-action

Follow-up:
- ✅ Send a timely thank-you note and recap of the discussion
- ✅ Adhere to the agreed-upon timeline for next steps
- ❌ Neglect to follow up or fail to deliver on promises

By following this checklist, sales reps at [COMPANY] can ensure a consistent, effective sales process that maximizes success rates and client satisfaction.
