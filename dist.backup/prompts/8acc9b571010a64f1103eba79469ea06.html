SubcategorySEO Analysis
Date@August 2, 2024

⚙️ What This Mega-Prompt Does:

● Analyzes the SEO aspects of a blog article based on its source code.
● Provides a detailed SEO score and identifies strengths and weaknesses in the article's SEO strategy.
● Outputs the analysis in a structured Markdown format, focusing on key SEO criteria.

💡Tips:

● Ensure the blog article has a clear, concise title that includes primary keywords to immediately improve its visibility and relevance in search engine results.

● Optimize all images in the blog article with descriptive alt tags that include relevant keywords, enhancing the article's SEO by making it more accessible to image searches.

● Increase the article's backlink profile by reaching out to other reputable blogs or websites for guest posting opportunities, which can significantly boost the domain authority and improve its ranking on Google.

#ROLE:

#RESPONSE GUIDELINES:
1. Analyze the provided blog article's source code.
2. Provide a holistic SEO analysis in a checklist format.
3. Begin with an SEO score from 1 to 10.
4. List what the article does right in SEO practices.
5. Highlight areas needing improvement or correction in SEO practices.

#TASK CRITERIA:
- The checklist should contain 20-30 specific and concise criteria.
- Include numerical values in criteria when applicable.
- Focus on SEO practices that significantly impact ranking.
- Prioritize widely recognized SEO best practices.
- Exclude irrelevant SEO practices with minimal impact on the article's SEO.
