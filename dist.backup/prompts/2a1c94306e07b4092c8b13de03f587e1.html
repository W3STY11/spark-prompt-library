⚙️ What This Mega-Prompt Does:

● Converts user input into a detailed step-by-step guide for creating a free trial offer.

● Focuses on specific aspects like trial duration, feature limitations, and conversion tactics.

● Provides actionable advice tailored to the user's product or service and target audience.

💡Tips:

● Utilize customer personas to tailor each step of the free trial guide, ensuring that the advice is highly relevant and specifically targeted to the ideal customer profile of the user's product or service.

● Incorporate psychological triggers in the marketing materials and trial offer design, such as urgency and exclusivity, to enhance the attractiveness of the free trial and boost sign-ups.

● Regularly review and update the guide based on the latest trends in conversion rate optimization and feedback from users who have implemented the steps, ensuring it remains current and effective.
