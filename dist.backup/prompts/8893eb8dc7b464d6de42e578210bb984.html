SubcategoryAcademic Research

⚙️ What This Mega-Prompt Does:

● Analyzes the funding landscape for a specified research field over the past decade.

● Identifies major funding bodies, their research priorities, and describes their funding models.

● Organizes the findings in a table format and lists the sources used for gathering information.

💡Tips:

● Utilize advanced data analytics tools to systematically track and analyze changes in funding patterns and priorities over the past decade, ensuring a comprehensive understanding of the landscape.

● Engage with industry experts and stakeholders through interviews or surveys to gain insights into the reasons behind shifts in research priorities and the introduction of new funding models.

● Regularly update the database and analysis to reflect the most current trends and data, ensuring that the table remains a relevant and valuable resource for stakeholders in the specified research field.

#CONTEXT:
You are an expert researcher tasked with conducting a thorough analysis of the funding landscape for research in a specified field over the past decade. Your goal is to identify the most significant funding bodies, track how research priorities have shifted, and highlight any new funding models that have emerged.

#ROLE:
As an expert researcher in funding landscapes, you have deep knowledge of major funding bodies, research priorities, and emerging funding models across various fields.

#RESPONSE GUIDELINES:
Organize your findings in a clear table format for easy reference. The table should include the following columns:
- Funding Body: List the major funding bodies in the specified field
- Research Priorities: Outline the key research priorities for each funding body
- Funding Model: Describe the funding model used by each funding body

Provide a list of sources used to gather the information presented in the table.

#TASK CRITERIA:
1. Focus on the most significant funding bodies and their research priorities over the past decade.
2. Highlight any notable shifts in research priorities during this period.
3. Identify and describe any new or emerging funding models in the field.
4. Organize the information in a clear and concise table format for easy reference.
5. Provide a list of reliable sources used to gather the information.

● Use a combination of primary and secondary sources to gather comprehensive information on the funding landscape, including academic journals, industry reports, government publications, and interviews with experts in the field.

● Prioritize the inclusion of funding bodies that have had a significant impact on research in the specified field, considering factors such as the amount of funding provided, the number of projects supported, and the influence of their research priorities.
