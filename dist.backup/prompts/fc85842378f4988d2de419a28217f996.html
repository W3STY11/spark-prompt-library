SubcategoryAffiliate Marketing
Date@August 6, 2025 (UTC)

⚙️ What This Prompt Does:

● Provides a structured approach to creating comprehensive FAQ content for affiliate product review pages.
● Anticipates and addresses genuine customer concerns to build trust and reinforce purchase decisions.
● Ensures the FAQ section is authentic, transparent, and conversion-optimized.

💡Tips:

● Identify the key concerns and doubts of your target audience to craft questions that resonate and build trust.

● Use specific details and transparent language to address questions, subtly reinforcing product benefits without overselling.

● Regularly update the FAQ section based on customer feedback and evolving product information to maintain relevance and trust.

#RESPONSE GUIDELINES:
1. Start with a brief introduction explaining the FAQ section's purpose
2. Generate 8-12 questions organized by buyer journey stage:
   - Trust/Credibility questions (2-3)
   - Product-specific concerns (3-4)
   - Purchase/shipping logistics (2-3)
   - Post-purchase support (1-2)
3. Each answer should:
   - Address the real concern behind the question
   - Include specific details that build trust
   - Subtly reinforce product benefits without overselling
   - Be 2-4 sentences maximum
4. Focus on authenticity over aggressive selling
5. Include questions that competitors avoid answering
6. Address affiliate relationship transparently

#FAQ CRITERIA:
1. Questions must sound like real customer concerns, not marketing setups
2. Avoid generic questions found on every product page
3. Include at least one "uncomfortable" question about product limitations
4. Address the affiliate relationship directly in at least one FAQ
5. Use conversational language, not corporate speak
6. Include specific details (timeframes, numbers, policies) when possible
7. Balance honesty with conversion optimization
8. Avoid superlatives and exaggerated claims
9. Include questions that demonstrate deep product knowledge
