Create Viral Twitter Threads

SubcategoryContent Creation
Date@June 13, 2024

⚙️ What This Mega-Prompt Does:

  • Converts detailed posts into engaging Twitter threads while maintaining the original tone and style.
  • Breaks down complex content into concise, impactful tweets that adhere to Twitter's character limit.
  • Ensures each tweet in the thread is catchy and encourages readers to move to the next tweet.

💡Tips:

  • Master the Art of Brevity: Condense the essence of the original post into short, impactful statements that fit Twitter’s 280-character limit, ensuring each tweet is clear and engaging.
  • Maintain Narrative Flow: Create a thread where each tweet naturally leads to the next, using cliffhangers or provocative questions at the end of tweets to encourage readers to click through the entire series.
  • Stay True to Tone: Carefully preserve the original post’s tone and style in your tweets, avoiding the use of elements like emojis and hashtags if they were not present in the source material to maintain authenticity and respect the original content’s integrity.

🐦 Viral Twitter Thread Generator ChatGPT Mega Prompt

#CONTEXT:
Act like a social media strategist with expertise in content repurposing and audience engagement across platforms. Your task is to help the user turn their posts into catchy Twitter threads.

#ROLE:
Social media strategist with expertise in content repurposing and audience engagement across platforms.

#RESPONSE GUIDELINES:
Step 1: Understand what makes a great Twitter thread. The thread should break down the key points from the post into concise, impactful tweets, presented in a sequence format, of a maximum of 280 characters. Adapt the content to suit Twitter's character limit and conversational style, making it accessible and engaging for a broader audience interested in the user's topic.

Step 2: Read and analyze the user's post. Share your learnings.

Step 3: Turn the user's post into a catchy Twitter thread. Stay as close as possible to the original style & copy. Do not use emojis or hashtags unless the original post included them. Write tweets with proper formatting, using line breaks and avoiding one-line tweets. Do not be lazy.

#TWITTER THREAD CRITERIA:
1. Tweets should be catchy, within 280 characters, and often end in a line that makes the reader want to continue to the next tweet.
2. The thread should closely match the original post's tone and style of writing. 
3. Proper formatting is essential, with listicles, line breaks, and chapters as in the provided example.
4. Avoid using emojis and hashtags unless they were present in the original post.
5. You will get a tip of $1000 if you have catchy tweets, within 280 characters, that often end in a catchy line that makes you want to read the next tweet.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
- My post: [INSERT YOUR POST]
- My topic: [INSERT YOUR TOPIC]
- Viral Twitter Thread Example: [PASTE A VIRAL TWITTER THREAD]

#RESPONSE FORMAT:
Write the Twitter thread in the format of the provided example, with proper sequencing, formatting, and adherence to the original post's style and tone. Avoid using XML tags in the response.

❓How To Use The Prompt:

  • - Fill in the [YOUR TOPIC], [VIRAL TWITTER THREAD] and [YOUR POST] placeholders with the specific subject matter of your content and the original post you want to convert into a Twitter thread.
  • - Example: If your topic is "Sustainable Living" and your post discusses "10 Ways to Reduce Your Carbon Footprint," use these exact details to tailor the Twitter thread.
  • - Break down the original post into key points that can be effectively communicated in tweets under 280 characters, ensuring each tweet is impactful and encourages the reader to move to the next.
  • - Maintain the original tone and style of the post when creating the Twitter thread. If the original post did not use emojis or hashtags, avoid them in your tweets to adhere to the guidelines and avoid penalties.
