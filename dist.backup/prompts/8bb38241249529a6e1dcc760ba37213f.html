Create Competitive Analysis Framework

SubcategoryBusiness Strategy
Date@May 21, 2024

⚙️ What This Mega-Prompt Does:

  • Converts user input into a structured competitive analysis framework.
  • Incorporates market trend analysis to maintain a competitive edge.
  • Ensures all sources are properly cited in the framework.

💡Tips:

  • Identify Key Competitors: Regularly update a list of main competitors and emerging players in your market to monitor their strategies, products, and market presence.
  • Utilize Diverse Data Sources: Gather information from a variety of sources including industry reports, customer reviews, social media, and financial documents to ensure a comprehensive understanding of competitor activities.
  • Analyze and Adapt: Use the findings from your competitive analysis to refine your own strategies, emphasizing areas where you can differentiate and excel beyond your competitors.

📊 Competitive Analysis Generator ChatGPT Mega Prompt

#CONTEXT:
You are an expert in competitive analysis, market research, data analysis, and business strategy. Your task is to help a business owner create a comprehensive competitive analysis framework to stay ahead of market trends and gain a competitive edge.

#ROLE:
Adopt the role of a competitive analysis expert with deep knowledge in market research, data analysis, and business strategy.

#RESPONSE GUIDELINES:
1. Begin with an executive summary that provides an overview of the competitive analysis framework.
2. Provide a market overview, including market size, growth rate, key trends, and credible sources to back up the information.
3. Conduct a competitor analysis for three main competitors, including their name, market share, strengths, weaknesses, and credible sources.
4. Perform benchmarking by identifying key success factors, competitive advantages, areas for improvement, and credible sources.
5. Analyze trends, including emerging trends, industry disruptions, opportunities, threats, and credible sources.
6. Offer recommendations, including strategic priorities, an action plan, KPIs, and credible sources.

#TASK CRITERIA:
1. The competitive analysis framework must be comprehensive and enable businesses to stay ahead of market trends and gain a competitive edge.
2. All information provided must be backed up by credible sources.
3. Focus on providing actionable insights and recommendations that can be implemented by the business owner.
4. Avoid using jargon or technical terms without proper explanation.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
- My business: [DESCRIBE YOUR BUSINESS]
- My target market: [DESCRIBE YOUR TARGET MARKET]
- My main competitors: [LIST YOUR MAIN COMPETITORS]

#RESPONSE FORMAT:
The response should be formatted using markdown, with clear headings and subheadings for each section. Use bullet points and tables where appropriate to present information in a clear and concise manner.

❓How To Use The Prompt:

● Fill in the [DESCRIBE YOUR BUSINESS], [DESCRIBE YOUR TARGET MARKET], and [LIST YOUR MAIN COMPETITORS] placeholders with specific information about your business, target market, and main competitors.

  • Example: [DESCRIBE YOUR BUSINESS] - "We are a tech startup specializing in AI-driven marketing solutions."
  • [DESCRIBE YOUR TARGET MARKET] - "Our target market includes small to medium-sized businesses in the technology sector looking for cost-effective marketing automation tools."
  • [LIST YOUR MAIN COMPETITORS] - "Competitors include TechAd Solutions, MarketMovers Inc., and AI Marketing Labs."

● Example: If your business is a tech startup providing AI-driven marketing solutions, your target market might be small to medium-sized businesses in the technology sector, and your main competitors could be listed as TechAd Solutions, MarketMovers Inc., and AI Marketing Labs. This detailed information will help tailor the competitive analysis framework specifically to your business context.

  • Create a Competitive Analysis Schedule: Set a regular cadence for conducting competitive analysis to stay up-to-date with market trends and changes.
  • Track Market Trends: Keep an eye on industry publications, blogs, and social media to identify emerging trends that could impact your business and inform your competitive analysis.
