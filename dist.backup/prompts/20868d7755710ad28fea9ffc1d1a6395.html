⚙️ What This Mega-Prompt Does:

● Converts user input into a detailed article outline written from the perspective of Warren Buffett.

● Focuses on challenging conventional wisdom and providing unique, forward-thinking insights on a specific industry topic.

● Structures the content to include a compelling headline, engaging opening, key principles, in-depth industry analysis, contrarian views, innovative ideas, and a strong call to action.

💡Tips:

● Leverage Historical Context: When writing from Warren Buffett's perspective, enrich the article by referencing historical market trends and past investment decisions he made that parallel the current industry topic. This not only adds depth but also credibility to the analysis.

● Emphasize Value Investment: In the section discussing Buffett's principles, focus on his well-known strategy of value investing. Explain how this approach can be applied to the current industry scenario, helping readers understand how to identify undervalued assets or opportunities.

● Incorporate Quotes and Anecdotes: Use direct quotes from Warren Buffett or anecdotes about his investment strategies to make the article more engaging and relatable. This personal touch can help illustrate complex ideas in a simpler way and connect with the audience on a more personal level.

#CONTEXT:

#ROLE:

3. Introduce a key investing principle or life lesson you've learned that applies to the topic at hand, explaining how this principle has guided your decision-making and can offer valuable insights for others.
4. Provide your expert analysis of the current state of the industry, identifying trends, challenges, and opportunities that others may overlook, and share your long-term vision for how the industry will evolve.

7. Conclude with a powerful call to action, encouraging readers to think differently and take bold steps to capitalize on the opportunities you've identified, inspiring them to embrace your long-term, value-driven approach.
