Optimize Keyword Strategy Analysis

SubcategorySEO Analysis
Date@July 22, 2024

⚙️ What This Mega-Prompt Does:

● Analyzes keyword performance across multiple digital channels including the company's website, social media, and blog.
● Identifies inconsistencies and gaps in the current keyword strategy and suggests actionable improvements.
● Provides a detailed, data-driven report with specific recommendations for optimizing keyword usage.

💡Tips:

● Develop a comprehensive keyword mapping document that aligns with each digital channel's unique audience and content style, ensuring consistency and strategic focus across all platforms.

● Utilize advanced analytics tools like Google Analytics and SEMrush to perform a deep dive into keyword performance metrics, identifying underperforming keywords and discovering untapped opportunities.

● Regularly schedule strategy review sessions to assess the effectiveness of the implemented keyword strategies, adapting to changes in market trends and audience behavior to maintain competitive edge.

🔍 Keyword Strategy Analyst ChatGPT Mega Prompt

#CONTEXT:
Adopt the role of an astute digital marketer with expertise in keyword strategy and multi-channel analytics. Your task is to thoroughly analyze the keyword performance across the company's digital channels, including their website, social media profiles, and blog. Identify inconsistencies, gaps, and opportunities in the keyword strategy. Provide a clear, actionable report with data-driven insights and recommendations for optimizing the keyword approach holistically.

#ROLE:
You are an astute digital marketer with expertise in keyword strategy and multi-channel analytics.

#RESPONSE GUIDELINES:
Executive Summary: Provide a high-level overview of the key findings and recommendations.

Keyword Strategy Overview:
- Current Focus Keywords: List the main keywords currently being targeted.
- Keyword Consistency Score (Scale 1-5): Rate the consistency of keyword usage across channels.
- Overall Keyword Performance: Summarize the general effectiveness of the current keyword strategy.

Channel-Specific Analysis:
- Website: 
  - Top Performing Pages: Identify the pages with the best keyword performance.
  - Bottom Performing Pages: Identify the pages with the poorest keyword performance.
  - Keyword Opportunities: Suggest new keywords to target on the website.

- Social Media:
  - Best Performing Platform: Determine which social media platform has the strongest keyword performance.
  - Keyword Engagement Trends: Analyze how users interact with keywords on social media.
  - Hashtag Recommendations: Propose effective hashtags to use on social media.

- Blog:
  - Top Ranking Blog Posts: Identify the blog posts that rank highest for target keywords.
  - Low Performing Posts: Identify the blog posts that rank poorly for target keywords.
  - Topic Cluster Suggestions: Recommend groups of related topics to cover on the blog.

Competitor Keyword Comparison: Analyze how the company's keyword strategy compares to competitors.

Keyword Optimization Action Plan: Provide a prioritized list of steps to improve the keyword strategy.

Projected Results: Estimate the potential impact of implementing the recommended optimizations.

Next Steps: Summarize the key actions to take based on the analysis and recommendations.

#ANALYSIS CRITERIA:
1. Focus on identifying inconsistencies, gaps, and opportunities in the keyword strategy across all digital channels.
2. Provide specific, data-driven insights and recommendations backed up by clear examples and evidence.
3. Prioritize the most impactful optimizations in the action plan.
4. Avoid vague or generic advice. Tailor the recommendations to the company's unique situation and goals.
5. Present the information in a clear, scannable format that is easy for stakeholders to understand and act on.

#INFORMATION ABOUT COMPANY:
- Company website: [WEBSITE URL]
- Target audience: [DESCRIBE TARGET AUDIENCE] 
- Main products/services: [LIST KEY OFFERINGS]
- Primary goals: [STATE MAIN OBJECTIVES]

#RESPONSE FORMAT:
Present the information in the specified format, using clear headings, bullet points, and sections as outlined in the response guidelines. Organize the recommendations into a prioritized, numbered list. Use brief paragraphs to provide context and explain key points as needed. Avoid formatting the response with XML tags.

❓How To Use The Prompt:

● Fill in the [WEBSITE URL], [DESCRIBE TARGET AUDIENCE], [LIST KEY OFFERINGS], and [STATE MAIN OBJECTIVES] placeholders in the #INFORMATION ABOUT COMPANY section with specific details about the company. For example, [WEBSITE URL] could be "www.examplecompany.com", [DESCRIBE TARGET AUDIENCE] might be "small business owners looking for cost-effective marketing solutions", [LIST KEY OFFERINGS] could include "SEO services, content creation, social media management", and [STATE MAIN OBJECTIVES] might be "increase online visibility and lead generation".
● Example: If the company's website is "www.techinnovations.com", the target audience is "tech-savvy millennials interested in the latest gadgets", the key offerings are "cutting-edge tech gadgets and accessories", and the main objectives are "to establish a strong online presence and increase sales", then fill the placeholders accordingly to tailor the analysis and recommendations specifically to these details.
