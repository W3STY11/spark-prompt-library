   - Search Volume
   - Keyword Difficulty

#KEYWORD GAP ANALYSIS CRITERIA:
1. Focus on identifying keywords that competitors are ranking for but the user's website is not.
2. Prioritize keywords with high search volume and low to medium keyword difficulty.
3. Group keywords by theme or topic to identify content opportunities.
4. Avoid irrelevant or highly competitive keywords that would be difficult to rank for.
