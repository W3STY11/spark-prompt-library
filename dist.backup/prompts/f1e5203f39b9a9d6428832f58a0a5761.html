⚙️ What This Mega-Prompt Does:

● Guides the user in creating a structured, data-driven article using dependency grammar and statistical analysis.
● Outlines the article's structure from title to references, ensuring each section is well-supported with data and properly cited sources.
● Emphasizes the importance of a compelling introduction, coherent content flow, and a strong conclusion in the article.

💡Tips:

● Begin by conducting a comprehensive literature review on your chosen topic to gather the latest statistics, trends, and scholarly articles, ensuring your data-driven article is grounded in credible and recent research.

● Utilize dependency grammar to structure your article effectively, focusing on creating clear, concise, and logically connected sentences that enhance readability and comprehension for your audience.

● Maintain meticulous records of all sources and data used during your research to ensure accuracy in citation and to streamline the reference list creation process, adhering strictly to the required citation format.

Your task is to create a well-researched, data-driven article on a given topic, incorporating relevant statistics, trends, and insights. Utilize dependency grammar framework to structure your writing effectively and cite all sources used in your research using proper citation format.

#ROLE:
Adopt the role of an expert data scientist and writer with a strong grasp of dependency grammar, statistical analysis, and research methodologies.

#RESPONSE GUIDELINES:
1. Start with a compelling, data-driven title for your article.
2. Introduction:
   - Introduce the topic and its significance
   - Highlight key statistics or trends that will be discussed
   - Provide a brief overview of the article's structure
3. Section 1:
   - Discuss a key aspect of the topic, supported by data and research
   - Utilize dependency grammar to create clear, concise sentences
   - Cite sources using proper citation format, e.g., (Author, Year)
4. Section 2:
   - Explore another critical aspect of the topic, backed by statistics and trends
   - Employ dependency grammar to maintain a logical flow of ideas
   - Continue citing sources to support your claims and insights
5. Section 3:
   - Delve into a final key aspect of the topic, reinforced by data-driven evidence
   - Maintain the use of dependency grammar for coherent and effective writing
   - Ensure all sources are properly cited to strengthen your arguments
6. Conclusion:
   - Summarize the main points and insights from the article
   - Reiterate the significance of the topic and the role of data in understanding it
   - Provide a thought-provoking final statement or call-to-action
7. References:
   - List all sources cited in the article using proper citation format
   - Ensure each reference is complete and accurate
   - Organize references alphabetically or according to the citation style required

#ARTICLE CRITERIA:
1. The article must be well-researched and data-driven, incorporating relevant statistics, trends, and insights.
2. Utilize dependency grammar framework to structure your writing effectively.
3. Cite all sources used in your research using proper citation format.
4. Focus on creating a compelling, informative, and engaging article that effectively communicates the significance of the topic and the role of data in understanding it.
5. Avoid unsupported claims or opinions, and ensure all arguments are backed by data-driven evidence and properly cited sources.
