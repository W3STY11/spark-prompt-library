⚙️ What This Mega-Prompt Does:

● Analyzes the provided viral post to identify key success factors such as emotional triggers, curiosity elements, and credibility signals.

● Creates a viral topic template that can be used to generate new content ideas based on the successful elements of the example post.

● Brainstorms 10 unique viral topics, detailing their core angles, viral potential, headline options, and main content points.

💡Tips:

● Analyze Competitor Engagement: Study the comments and shares on the original viral post to understand what specifically resonated with the audience. This can provide insights into emotional triggers and engagement tactics that can be replicated or improved in your content.

● Develop a Unique Twist: Use the viral topic template to inject a unique twist into your content that differentiates it from the original post. This could involve a counterintuitive stance, an unexpected perspective, or a deeper dive into a particular aspect that was only briefly touched in the original.

● Test Headline Variants: Before finalizing your content, test multiple headline options using tools like CoSchedule's Headline Analyzer to determine which ones are most compelling. This helps ensure that the headlines not only draw attention but also align well with the content's core message and emotional appeal.

🔥 Viral Content Generator ChatGPT Mega Prompt

#CONTEXT:
You are a content creator looking to create viral content by remixing existing successful posts in your niche. You have provided an example of a viral post and need help analyzing what made it successful and generating new ideas based on those key factors.

#ROLE:
Content strategy expert specializing in viral content creation

#RESPONSE GUIDELINES:
1. Dissect the provided viral post and identify the key factors that contributed to its success, such as:
   - Emotional buttons it pushes
   - Anticipation/curiosity it sparks
   - Bold/surprising angles it takes
   - Specificity of claims/examples
   - Credibility signals it uses
   - "Curiosity gaps" it opens

2. Create a "viral topic template" based on the analysis that can be used to generate new, successful content ideas.

3. Brainstorm 10 unique viral topics based on the key elements uncovered in the analysis. For each idea, include:
   - The core topic/angle
   - Why it has huge viral potential based on the analysis
   - 2-3 irresistible headline options
   - A quick outline of main points to cover

4. Focus on topics that feel fresh and provocative while still pushing the same "share buttons" as the original post. Avoid personal content ideas.

#VIRAL CONTENT CRITERIA:
1. The content should remix the original topic into something that feels new and noteworthy.
2. The ideas should push the same emotional buttons, spark curiosity, and take bold angles as the original post.
3. Each idea should have a clear core topic or angle and be supported by specific claims and examples.
4. The headlines should be irresistible and encourage users to click and share.
5. The main points should be well-structured and provide a comprehensive overview of the topic.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
- My niche: [INSERT YOUR NICHE]
- Viral post example: [INSERT VIRAL POST]

#RESPONSE FORMAT:
1. Viral Post Analysis
   - Key success factors in bullet points

2. Viral Topic Template
   - A clear, concise template for generating viral content ideas

3. 10 Viral Topic Ideas
   For each idea:
   - Core topic/angle
   - Viral potential explanation
   - 2-3 headline options
   - Quick outline of main points

💡Additional Tips:

● Leverage Social Listening: Monitor conversations and trends in your niche on social media platforms to identify popular topics and themes that have the potential to go viral. This can help you stay ahead of the curve and create content that resonates with your target audience.

● Incorporate Visual Elements: Visual content tends to perform better and have a higher chance of going viral. Consider incorporating eye-catching images, videos, or infographics into your content to make it more engaging and shareable.

● Optimize for Shareability: Make it easy for users to share your content by including social sharing buttons on your website or blog. Additionally, craft compelling calls-to-action that encourage readers to share your content with their networks.

● Collaborate with Influencers: Partnering with influencers in your niche can significantly increase the reach and virality of your content. Identify influencers who align with your brand and collaborate with them to create and promote your content to their followers.

● Monitor and Analyze Performance: Keep track of the performance of your viral content to understand what works and what doesn't. Analyze metrics such as engagement, shares, and comments to gain insights and refine your content strategy for future success.
