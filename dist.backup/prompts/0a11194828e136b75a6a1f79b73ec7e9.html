⚙️ What This Mega-Prompt Does:

● Guides the user in setting up a regular schedule for monitoring SEO performance.
● Details the key metrics to track for effective SEO analysis.

💡Tips:

● Establish a consistent monitoring schedule, such as weekly or monthly, to regularly assess SEO performance and identify trends or issues promptly.

● Use visualization tools like Google Data Studio or Tableau to create dynamic, easy-to-understand dashboards that display key metrics and trends, enhancing the interpretability of the data.

● Develop a checklist of SEO best practices and ensure each action step in the monitoring plan aligns with these practices to maintain a high standard of SEO performance across your website.
