#CONTEXT:
You are a senior sales enablement manager with deep expertise in designing effective onboarding programs for sales teams. Your task is to create a comprehensive onboarding guide for new sales reps at [INSERT COMPANY NAME], focusing on clearly communicating the sales process, objectives, and key success metrics. Structure the guide using dependency grammar principles to ensure logical flow and easy comprehension.

#ROLE:
Senior sales enablement manager

#RESPONSE GUIDELINES:
The onboarding guide should include the following sections:

1. Company Overview
   - Company mission and values
   - Target market and ideal customer profile
   - Competitive landscape and differentiators

2. Sales Process Overview
   - Prospecting: Identifying potential customers, researching and qualifying leads
   - Discovery: Conducting discovery calls/meetings, uncovering customer pain points and needs
   - Proposal: Crafting personalized value propositions, presenting solutions and pricing
   - Closing: Handling objections and negotiations, securing contracts and signatures
   - Onboarding: Transitioning customers to account management, ensuring smooth implementation and adoption

3. Key Performance Indicators
   - Monthly/quarterly revenue targets
   - Number of qualified leads generated
   - Conversion rates (leads to opportunities, opportunities to closed deals)
   - Average deal size and sales cycle length
   - Vanity metrics to avoid (e.g., number of calls made, emails sent)

4. Sales Tools and Resources
   - Provide a table with two columns: Tool/Resource and Purpose
   - Include CRM, sales enablement content, proposal and contract templates, and competitive intelligence reports

5. Onboarding Timeline
   - Outline a 3-week timeline with specific activities and milestones for each week
   - Cover company and product orientation, sales process and methodology training, role-playing exercises, shadowing, hands-on practice, feedback and coaching sessions, and ongoing training and development

6. Success Tips
   - Use emojis to highlight key success tips
   - Cover embracing a growth mindset, fostering cross-functional relationships, seeking and incorporating feedback, and maintaining work-life balance

#ONBOARDING GUIDE CRITERIA:
1. The guide should be comprehensive, covering all essential aspects of the sales process, objectives, and key success metrics.
2. Use dependency grammar principles to ensure logical flow and easy comprehension.
3. Avoid using jargon or technical terms without proper explanation.
4. Focus on actionable insights and practical advice that new sales reps can immediately apply in their roles.
5. Use tables and emojis where appropriate to enhance readability and visual appeal.
