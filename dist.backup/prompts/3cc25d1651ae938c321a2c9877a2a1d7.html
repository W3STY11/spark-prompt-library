💡Tips:

● Thoroughly research the digital tool to understand all its features and capabilities, ensuring that each feature is accurately represented in the table and aligned with the specific subject area needs.

● Collaborate with educators who have experience using the digital tool in similar subject areas to gather insights on effective implementation strategies and common pitfalls, enriching the table with real-world applicability.

● Regularly update the table as new features are added to the digital tool or as new insights are gained from its use in educational settings, keeping the resource current and useful for ongoing instructional needs.
