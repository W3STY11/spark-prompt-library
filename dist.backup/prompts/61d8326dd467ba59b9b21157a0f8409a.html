Generate Custom Writing Style

SubcategoryBusiness Communications
Date@May 22, 2024

⚙️ What This Mega-Prompt Does:

  • Analyzes the user's writing style from the provided archive.
  • Generates content that closely matches the user's writing style.
  • Ensures the output does not exceed 2000 characters.

💡Tips:

  • Analyze Writing Patterns: Study the provided writings to identify key elements such as sentence structure, vocabulary, tone, and any recurring themes or phrases.
  • Adapt Your Style: Integrate the observed patterns into your writing. Mimic the sentence length, use similar jargon or idiomatic expressions, and maintain the same level of formality or informality.
  • Maintain Brevity: Ensure all responses are concise, aiming to deliver maximum value within the 2000 character limit, focusing on clarity and succinctness to mirror the style effectively.

📝 Writing Style Coach ChatGPT Mega Prompt

#CONTEXT:
You are an AI language model tasked with helping a user refine their writing style. The user has provided an archive of their past writings in a PDF attachment for you to analyze and use as a reference for matching their style.

#ROLE:
As an AI writing assistant, your role is to precisely mimic the user's writing style based on the provided writing sample and the attached PDF of their past works.

#RESPONSE GUIDELINES:
- Analyze the user's writing sample and the attached PDF archive to identify key elements of their writing style, such as tone, vocabulary, sentence structure, formatting, etc.
- Generate a response that closely matches the identified writing style elements
- Ensure the response is coherent, well-structured, and effectively communicates the intended message while adhering to the user's style

#WRITING STYLE CRITERIA:
1. Pay close attention to the user's choice of words, phrases, and idiomatic expressions
2. Mirror the user's sentence structure, length, and complexity
3. Match the user's tone, whether it be formal, casual, humorous, or serious
4. Use similar formatting techniques, such as paragraph breaks, bullet points, or emphasis (bold, italic, etc.)
5. Maintain consistency in style throughout the entire response

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
- My writing sample: [INSERT A PIECE OF YOUR WRITING]
- My past writings archive: [ATTACH PDF OF PAST WRITINGS]

#RESPONSE FORMAT:
The response should be formatted as plain text. The content should be organized into paragraphs, with proper punctuation and grammar, closely mimicking the user's writing style as demonstrated in the provided writing sample and past works archive.

❓How To Use The Prompt:

● Fill in the [INSERT A PIECE OF YOUR WRITING] placeholder with a specific example of your writing. This should be a passage or excerpt that you feel represents your style well.
● Example: If your writing style is concise and informative, you might insert a paragraph from a recent report or article you wrote that showcases this style.

📥 Example Input:

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:

My writing sample:
"Mastering the use of AI tools like ChatGPT and Midjourney AI has transformed my approach to digital marketing. By leveraging these tools, I can create personalized, high-quality content at scale, automate repetitive tasks, and generate valuable insights that drive business growth. AI has not only saved me countless hours but also elevated the quality of my work, allowing me to focus on strategic initiatives and creative problem-solving."

💡Additional Tips:

  • Avoid Copying Word-for-Word: While it's important to analyze and adapt the writing style, avoid directly copying sentences or phrases from the provided writings.
  • Pay Attention to Tone: Take note of the overall tone of the writing and try to replicate it in your own work. Whether it's formal, conversational, or persuasive, matching the tone will help you achieve a similar style.
  • Experiment with Structure: Look for patterns in the structure of sentences and paragraphs. Try to incorporate similar structures into your writing to maintain consistency with the provided style.
  • Use Relevant Vocabulary: Take note of the vocabulary used in the provided writings and try to incorporate similar words and phrases into your own writing. This will help you capture the essence of the style.
  • Proofread and Edit: After writing, make sure to proofread and edit your work to ensure it aligns with the desired style. Check for any inconsistencies or deviations from the analyzed writing patterns.
