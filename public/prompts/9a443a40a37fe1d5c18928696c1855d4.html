#RESPONSE GUIDELINES:

   - Acknowledge their busy schedule, reiterate the key points from your previous email, provide additional value (e.g., case study, testimonial, exclusive offer), include a strong call-to-action to schedule a meeting or call, and close with a line emphasizing the urgency and signature.
