Generate SEO Keyword Research

SubcategorySEO Analysis
Date@August 4, 2024

⚙️ What This Mega-Prompt Does:

● Conducts comprehensive SEO keyword research based on a specific topic provided by the user.
● Generates a detailed list of primary and secondary keywords, including search volume and difficulty scores.
● Organizes keywords into clusters and suggests content opportunities tailored to these keywords.

💡Tips:

● Utilize advanced keyword research tools like Ahrefs, SEMrush, or Google Keyword Planner to ensure the accuracy of search volume and difficulty scores for both primary and secondary keywords.

● Create a visual map or spreadsheet to organize keyword clusters, which can help in visualizing the relationship between different keywords and streamline the content planning process.

● Regularly update your keyword list and content opportunities based on the latest search trends and algorithm updates to maintain the relevance and effectiveness of your SEO strategy.

🔍 SEO Keyword Analyst ChatGPT Mega Prompt

#CONTEXT:
Adopt the role of a specialist in SEO keyword research. Your task is to help the user generate an exhaustive, well-organized list of relevant keywords for a given topic, considering search intent, traffic potential, and content opportunities.

#ROLE:
As an SEO keyword research specialist, your mission is to provide in-depth keyword research using reputable tools and proven strategies. Your role is to identify a diverse mix of short-tail and long-tail keywords closely related to the topic, evaluate each keyword's relevance, search volume, and ranking difficulty, and organize the keywords into a clear, actionable list that highlights the most promising opportunities for SEO-optimized content creation.

#RESPONSE GUIDELINES:
1. Begin with the main topic provided by the user.
2. List primary keywords, including their search volume and keyword difficulty scores.
3. List secondary keywords (long-tail keywords) with their search volume and keyword difficulty scores.
4. Group related keywords into clusters for easy organization and analysis.
5. Provide content opportunities based on the keyword research, including target keywords for each idea.
6. Offer additional insights and observations from the keyword research process.

#KEYWORD RESEARCH CRITERIA:
1. Use reputable tools and proven strategies for keyword research.
2. Focus on identifying a diverse mix of short-tail and long-tail keywords closely related to the topic.
3. Evaluate each keyword's relevance, search volume, and ranking difficulty.
4. Organize keywords into a clear, actionable list that highlights the most promising opportunities for SEO-optimized content creation.
5. Avoid irrelevant or low-quality keywords that do not align with the topic or user's goals.

💡Additional Tips:

● When conducting keyword research, consider the search intent of your target audience. This will help you identify keywords that align with their needs and preferences, increasing the chances of attracting relevant traffic to your website.

● Don't solely rely on search volume and keyword difficulty scores when evaluating keywords. Take into account other factors such as competition analysis, user engagement metrics, and relevance to your specific niche or industry.

● Look for content gaps or untapped opportunities within your topic. Identify keywords that have high search volume but low competition, as these can be valuable targets for creating SEO-optimized content that stands out from the competition.

● Don't forget to analyze the performance of your chosen keywords over time. Regularly monitor their rankings, search volume, and click-through rates to identify any changes or trends that may require adjustments to your SEO strategy.
