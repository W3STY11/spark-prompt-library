⚙️ What This Prompt Does:

● Provides a comprehensive assessment of family structure, assets, debts, and long-term goals to tailor estate planning.

● Recommends essential estate planning tools like wills, trusts, and power of attorney to protect assets and honor wishes.

● Identifies critical gaps in current planning that expose the user to risk, offering prioritized action steps.

💡Tips:

● Define clear objectives for estate planning, ensuring they align with personal values and family needs.

● Conduct a thorough analysis of state-specific requirements and tax implications to optimize planning.

● Regularly review and update estate plans to reflect changes in family dynamics and asset portfolios.
