- List 3 specific challenges the client is facing

- Create a table comparing current costs to projected costs with the proposed solution, highlighting potential savings

- Use checkmarks to list 3 additional benefits of the proposed solution

- Outline a 4-step plan for implementing the proposed solution

- Summarize the compelling reasons for the client to adopt the product/service, emphasizing cost savings, benefits, and seamless implementation
- Provide a clear call-to-action

4. Avoid using jargon or technical terms without clear explanations
5. Maintain a professional and persuasive tone throughout the proposal
