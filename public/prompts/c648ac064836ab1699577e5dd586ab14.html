● Implement a lighting strategy that combines natural light with adjustable artificial lighting options, allowing individuals to customize light levels according to their comfort and the task at hand.

● Introduce biophilic design elements such as indoor plants, natural light, and materials that mimic nature to enhance relaxation and reduce stress levels in the work environment.

● Consider incorporating standing desks or adjustable desks that allow users to switch between sitting and standing positions throughout the day, promoting better posture and reducing the risk of musculoskeletal issues.

● Invest in high-quality ergonomic chairs that provide proper support for the back, neck, and arms, ensuring optimal comfort and reducing the strain on the body during long periods of sitting.

● Create designated quiet zones or soundproof areas where employees can retreat to for focused work or relaxation, minimizing distractions and promoting a calm and peaceful environment.
