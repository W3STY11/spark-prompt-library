💡Additional Tips:

● Use SMART criteria when setting goals: Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Relevant, and Time-bound. This helps to create clear and actionable goals that are more likely to be achieved.

● Break down big goals into smaller, manageable tasks: Breaking down goals into smaller steps makes them less overwhelming and increases motivation by providing a sense of progress.

● Set stretch goals that align with personal values and interests: When goals are aligned with personal values and interests, individuals are more likely to be motivated and committed to achieving them.

● Regularly track progress and celebrate milestones: Tracking progress helps to stay on track and identify areas for improvement. Celebrating milestones along the way boosts motivation and provides a sense of accomplishment.

● Seek support and accountability: Share goals with others and seek support from mentors, coaches, or accountability partners. This helps to stay motivated and accountable throughout the goal-setting journey.
