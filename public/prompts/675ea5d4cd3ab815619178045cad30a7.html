⚙️ What This Prompt Does:

● Transforms AI-generated content into natural, human-readable material.
● Ensures content passes human detection systems by using active voice and conversational flow.
● Maintains factual accuracy and proper attribution with credible sourcing.

💡Tips:

● Use active voice to make the content more engaging and relatable, ensuring it connects with the audience on a personal level.

● Replace banned words with natural alternatives that preserve the original meaning, enhancing the content's readability and appeal.

● Integrate short transitional phrases to guide readers smoothly through the content, maintaining their interest and understanding.
