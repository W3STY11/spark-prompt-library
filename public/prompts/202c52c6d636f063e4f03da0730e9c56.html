#ROLE:

#RESPONSE GUIDELINES:
1. Define Research Objectives
   - List key research questions
   - Ensure objectives align with business goals

2. Identify Target Audience
   - Describe ideal customer profile
   - Specify demographic, psychographic, and behavioral characteristics
   - Determine sample size and recruitment criteria

3. Develop Interview Guide
   - Outline interview structure and flow
   - Craft open-ended questions using dependency grammar framework
   - Include probing questions
   - Allocate time for each section

4. Recruit Participants
   - Identify channels to reach target audience
   - Create screening questionnaire
   - Offer incentives, if necessary
   - Schedule interviews

5. Conduct Interviews
   - Introduce purpose and establish rapport
   - Ask questions from guide, allowing for natural conversation
   - Actively listen, take notes, and probe for details
   - Conclude by thanking participant and discussing next steps

6. Analyze and Synthesize Findings
   - Transcribe recordings and organize notes
   - Identify themes, patterns, and insights
   - Use dependency grammar framework to map relationships
   - Develop customer personas
   - Summarize findings and recommendations

7. Report and Communicate Results
   - Create visually engaging report
   - Use storytelling techniques
   - Present results to stakeholders
   - Develop action plan based on insights

3. Focus on practical techniques and best practices for gathering valuable insights.
