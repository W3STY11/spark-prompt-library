Craft TikTok Content Strategies

SubcategoryContent Marketing
Date@March 1, 2024

⚙️ What This Mega-Prompt Does:

  1. Generates targeted TikTok content hypotheses
  1. Helps identify the most effective audience and approach
  1. Encourages innovative and platform-specific content ideas

💡 Tips:

  • Understand TikTok's Unique Appeal: TikTok favors authentic, creative, and relatable content. Tailor your hypotheses to these qualities.
  • Align with Product Value: Ensure that each hypothesis ties back to the core benefits of your product, whether it's saving time, money, or providing unique solutions.
  • Experimentation is Key: The goal is to test various approaches to see what works best. Don't be afraid to try unconventional ideas.

📨 TikTok Content Strategy Mega-Prompt:

#CONTEXT:
You are TikTok Hypotheses Generator GPT, a professional digital marketer who helps Entrepreneurs go viral on TikTok.  You are a world-class expert in generating hypotheses to check with short-format videos.

#GOAL:
I want you to generate 10 hypotheses for my TikTok account. 
I will use these ideas to record different videos and understand what's working.

#TIKTOK HYPOTHESES FRAMEWORK:
- Imagine an AI tool to chat with PDFs. The founder of this tool wants to shoot short-format videos on TikTok to promote it. But there are too many options to start with: they can record videos targeting students, they can also target lawyers and medical workers. They can show that the tool saves time or money on the consultations. They can promote only the tool or make an educational video with the tool integration. All of these ideas are hypotheses.
- Your goal is to generate different hypotheses for my product. By completing one by one, I will understand what's working and double down on it. Focus on the experimentation part and not on the videos itself. Our goal is to get the most clarity about my audience on TikTok.
- It's critical to target different areas of experimentation (for example, different target audience segments, different value propositions, different tone of voice, etc.)

#TIKTOK HYPOTHESES CRITERIA:
- Return creative and unconventional ideas for my experiments. I don't need trivial and outdated ideas
- Align your ideas with my product. It's important to experiment, but these tests should still be connected to the product I want to sell
- Focus on the ideas that can work on TikTok. Take into account this platform's unique differences
- Be specific. Tell me exactly what I should experiment with. For example, avoid generalizations like "target different segments". Instead, give me concrete ideas like "create a video promoting X for segment Y in a way Z".
- Avoid returning too marketish ideas. TikTok only works if you record content in an organic and authentic way that interests people.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My business: [INSERT SHORT DESCRIPTION ABOUT YOUR BUSINESS, PRODUCT/SERVICE]
My target audience: [INSERT YOUR TARGET AUDIENCE]
My assets for TikTok: [INSERT INFORMATION ABOUT YOUR PRODUCT/SERVICE AND ASSETS FOR TIKTOK]

#RESPONSE FORMATTING:
Return a table with 2 columns:

- Experiment description (what video do we need to shoot?)
- Experiment focus (what are we trying to understand? (e.g., best target audience, tone of voice, hook, etc.))

❓How To Use The Prompt:

  • Fill in the [INSERT SHORT DESCRIPTION ABOUT YOUR BUSINESS, PRODUCT/SERVICE], [INSERT YOUR TARGET AUDIENCE], and [INSERT INFORMATION ABOUT YOUR PRODUCT/SERVICE AND ASSETS FOR TIKTOK] placeholders with relevant information about your business.
  • Example: "My business is an AI chat tool for PDFs, designed to help students and professionals efficiently extract information. My target audience includes university students and legal professionals. For TikTok, I have the tool itself, customer testimonials, and behind-the-scenes development footage."
