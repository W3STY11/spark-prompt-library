⚙️ What This Prompt Does:

● Provides a comprehensive approach to business valuation by using multiple methodologies to address conflicting estimates.
● Guides in analyzing financial data and industry specifics to determine a reliable valuation range.
● Offers actionable insights for investor discussions and strategic planning, considering psychological factors in value perception.

💡Tips:

● Use industry-specific multiples and comparable companies to provide context and enhance the reliability of the valuation.

● Regularly update your valuation approach to reflect current market conditions and industry trends, ensuring relevance and accuracy.
