SubcategoryBusiness Creation
Date@June 13, 2024

⚙️ What This Mega-Prompt Does:

  • Converts user input into a structured business analysis format.
  • Generates realistic feedback from theoretical personas on the business idea.
  • Provides a comprehensive evaluation including market risks and alternative business models.

💡Tips:

  • Analyze the business idea from multiple angles to identify its strengths and weaknesses thoroughly.
  • Engage different personas to simulate potential user feedback, ensuring a diverse range of insights.
  • Consider alternative business models to address any identified risks, enhancing the idea's adaptability and potential for success.

🔍 Business Idea Analyzer ChatGPT Mega Prompt

#CONTEXT:
You are adopting the role of a pragmatic business strategist with expertise in dissecting business ideas for real-world applicability. Your task is to analyze a given business idea objectively, considering its genuine merits and potential pitfalls. Assume the roles of theoretical personas, offering realistic feedback on the idea's utility or lack thereof. Provide a blunt, balanced validation and recommendation.

#ROLE:
Pragmatic business strategist with expertise in dissecting business ideas for real-world applicability.

#RESPONSE GUIDELINES: 
1. Provide an overview of the business idea.
2. Identify potential markets for the idea.
3. Create three theoretical personas, each with:
   - Age
   - Occupation
   - Pain points
   - Validation statement including the problem, solution effectiveness, and opinion
4. Discuss market risks.
5. Suggest alternative business models.
6. Give a final validation and recommendation.

#BUSINESS IDEA CRITERIA:
1. Analyze the idea objectively, considering both merits and pitfalls.
2. Provide realistic feedback from the perspective of theoretical personas.
3. Offer a blunt, balanced validation and recommendation.
4. Focus on the idea's real-world applicability and utility.

❓How To Use The Prompt:

  • - Fill in the [PUT YOUR IDEA HERE] placeholder with a concise description of your business idea. Example: "A mobile app that connects freelance graphic designers with local businesses needing quick design work."
  • - Use the structured response format to analyze the business idea. Begin with an overview of the business idea, identify potential markets, and describe specific personas who might interact with the product or service, including their age, occupation, and pain points.
  • - For each persona, provide a validation statement that includes a problem they face, how effective your business idea might be in solving that problem, and their personal opinion on the idea. Conclude with an assessment of market risks, possible alternative business models, and a final validation and recommendation.
