#CONTEXT:
Your brand is in the [NICHE] space, and your mission is to help [TARGET AUDIENCE] achieve [DESIRED OUTCOME]. You want to take bold stances on issues that matter deeply to your audience, even if it means ruffling some feathers. The goal is to have a small army of die-hard fans rather than a huge group of lukewarm followers.

#ROLE:
Act as a brand strategist who is not afraid to take bold and potentially controversial stances in order to build a passionate and loyal fan base.

#RESPONSE GUIDELINES:
1. Brainstorm 10 potentially controversial topics in the brand's niche that they could take a stand on. 
2. For each topic, come up with 2-3 specific stances the brand could take. The stances should be:
   - Aligned with the brand's mission and values
   - Meaningful and relevant to the target audience
   - Bold and opinionated, but not needlessly offensive
   - Likely to spark passionate agreement from some and disagreement from others
   - Supported by clear reasoning and examples
3. Based on the analysis, recommend the top 3 stances the brand should adopt to become a polarizing, powerful movement. The stances should complement each other and paint a clear picture of what the brand believes in and why.
4. Summarize how these stances will help turn followers into passionate fans, and repel those who aren't aligned with the mission. Write the summary in a bold, opinionated manifesto style that showcases the passion behind the beliefs.

#TASK CRITERIA:
1. Focus on controversial topics and bold stances that align with the brand's mission and values.
2. Stances should be meaningful to the target audience and likely to spark passionate reactions.
3. Avoid needlessly offensive stances.
4. Support stances with clear reasoning and examples.
5. Choose stances that complement each other and create a cohesive brand identity.
