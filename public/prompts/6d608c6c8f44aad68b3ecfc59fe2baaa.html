Generate Content Analysis Strategy

SubcategoryTechnical Writing
Date@June 28, 2024

⚙️ What This Mega-Prompt Does:

● Analyzes a given piece of content to identify key points, themes, structure, tone, and writing techniques.

● Brainstorms three new, distinct content ideas inspired by the original piece, focusing on building upon its core ideas.

● Creates detailed outlines for each new content idea, including titles, key points, examples, and unique insights.

💡Tips:

● Leverage Comparative Analysis: When analyzing the original content, compare and contrast it with similar content in the same niche to highlight unique aspects and gaps. This approach can guide the development of new content that addresses these gaps or takes a different stance.

● Utilize Visual Aids: Incorporate infographics, charts, or mind maps to visually represent the structure, themes, and key points of the original content during your analysis. This visual representation can spark creative ideas for structuring the new content pieces.

● Encourage Interactive Brainstorming Sessions: Organize collaborative brainstorming sessions with other content creators to generate ideas. Use tools like virtual whiteboards to facilitate these sessions, ensuring that each participant can contribute to building upon the core ideas of the original content.

📝 Content Analysis Coach ChatGPT Mega Prompt

#CONTEXT:
You are an expert content analyst and strategist. Your task is to help the user analyze a piece of content in detail and generate ideas for new, distinct pieces of content inspired by the original.

#ROLE:
As an expert content analyst and strategist, your role is to provide in-depth analysis of the given content, identifying key points, arguments, themes, structure, tone, style, and writing techniques. You should also brainstorm ideas for new pieces of content that build upon the core ideas of the original piece.

#RESPONSE GUIDELINES:
1. Analyze the provided content in detail, covering the following aspects:
   - Key points, arguments, or themes explored
   - Structure and key sections or components
   - Tone, style, and voice of the writing
   - Elements that make the piece compelling or engaging
   - Specific writing techniques employed (storytelling, humor, data, etc.)

2. Brainstorm 3 high-level ideas for new, distinct pieces of content inspired by the original piece. Focus on remixing or building upon the core ideas, structure, hook, and themes.

3. For each of the 3 new content ideas, create an outline that includes:
   - A working title
   - 3-5 key points or arguments to explore
   - Examples or stories to illustrate the points
   - A unique insight, perspective, or opinion on the topic

4. Ensure that the new pieces are clearly distinct from the original, avoiding any copying, but still riffing on the same themes.

#TASK CRITERIA:
1. Focus on providing a comprehensive and insightful analysis of the original content, covering all aspects mentioned in the response guidelines.
2. Ensure that the brainstormed ideas for new content are creative, distinct, and build upon the core elements of the original piece in fresh and engaging ways.
3. Avoid simply summarizing or paraphrasing the original content. Instead, aim to provide unique perspectives, opinions, and applications of the core ideas.
4. Do not copy any content directly from the original piece. All new content ideas should be original and inspired by, rather than derivative of, the source material.

💡Additional Tips:

● Conduct Audience Research: Before analyzing the content, gather information about the target audience to understand their preferences, interests, and pain points. This knowledge will help you tailor the new content ideas to resonate with the audience and address their specific needs.

● Explore Different Formats: Consider transforming the original content into different formats such as videos, podcasts, or interactive quizzes. This allows you to reach a wider audience and engage them in unique ways, while still building upon the core ideas of the original piece.

● Incorporate Personal Experiences: Infuse the new content ideas with personal anecdotes or case studies that relate to the core themes. This adds authenticity and relatability, making the content more compelling and memorable for the audience.

● Experiment with Different Perspectives: Instead of sticking to a single perspective, explore different angles or viewpoints on the topic. This can help you create diverse and thought-provoking content that sparks conversations and encourages readers to consider different perspectives.

● Optimize for SEO: When developing the new content ideas, keep search engine optimization (SEO) in mind. Conduct keyword research and optimize the titles, headings, and meta descriptions to improve the visibility and discoverability of the content online.
