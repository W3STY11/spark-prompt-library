⚙️ What This Mega-Prompt Does:

  • Provides a structured approach to developing a comprehensive article outline.
  • Guides in writing a long-form, SEO-optimized article with engaging content.
  • Ensures the article meets specific SEO and readability criteria.

💡 Tips:

  • Outline Creation: Begin with brainstorming headings and subheadings around the chosen keyword. Ensure they are detailed and cover all aspects of the topic. Use tools like keyword planners for LSI keywords inspiration.
  • Type Continue: Just type “Continue” or “Continue writing the [Section]” to continue creating your article.
  • Type Expand: Just type “Expand writing the [Section]” or “Expand writing the [Section] with [Specific Information to Include]” in order to make a particular section longer.
  • SEO and Readability: Focus on the keyword density and placement, ensuring it appears in the first 10% of the content, in subheadings, and maintains a 1.30 density. Use simple, engaging language suitable for Grade 7 English.
  • Engagement and Flow: Incorporate various elements like lists, tables, and FAQs to keep the reader engaged. Use idioms, transitional phrases, and a conversational tone for a human-like style.
