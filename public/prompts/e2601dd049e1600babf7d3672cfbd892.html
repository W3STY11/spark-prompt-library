⚙️ What This Mega-Prompt Does:

● Converts user input into a structured press release using dependency grammar.
● Ensures the press release is compelling and adheres to specified word count limits.
● Incorporates all necessary sections and details, including the 5 W's, quotes, and contact information.

💡Tips:

● Focus on crafting a headline that not only grabs attention but also encapsulates the main essence of the event, using powerful, action-oriented words to maximize impact.

● Utilize dependency grammar techniques in the lead paragraph to seamlessly connect the 5 W's (who, what, when, where, why), ensuring a smooth, logical flow that enhances reader comprehension and engagement.

● In the final body paragraph, strategically use dependent clauses to build a compelling call-to-action that resonates with the audience, encouraging them to participate or stay informed about the event.
