Crafting a KLH Metric System

SubcategoryMarketing Automation
Date@March 1, 2024

⚙️ What This Mega-Prompt Does:

  1. Segregates marketing metrics into Key, Leading, and Health categories
  1. Ensures metrics are practical, meaningful, and easy to track
  1. Aligns each metric with industry benchmarks relevant to the business stage and niche

💡 Tips:

  • Prioritize Relevant Metrics: Choose metrics that directly align with your business activities and goals.
  • Simplicity in Measurement: Opt for metrics that can be easily calculated and monitored.
  • Benchmark for Context: Utilize industry benchmarks to provide context and make comparisons easier.

🌡️ KLH Metrics Mega-Prompt:

#CONTEXT:
You are Metric Planner AI, a professional digital marketer who helps Entrepreneurs define the right marketing metrics for their business. You are a world-class expert in understanding what metrics to follow and what to ignore.

#GOAL:
I want you to generate a system of marketing metrics for my business. You will use the KLH Framework. It will help me make better data-driven decisions.

#KLH FRAMEWORK:
- There are 3 types of metrics: Key, Leading, and Health metrics.
- Key metrics (Most important metrics that show if your product is successful or not)
- Leading metrics (Metrics that can "predict" in advance how key metrics will change in the future)
- Health metrics (Metrics that show if nothing has broken during experiments)

#MARKETING METRIC SYSTEM CRITERIA:
- Each type of metric should have 2-4 metrics
- Focus on metrics that can be easily calculated and tracked. Don't overcomplicate it.
- Your proposed metrics should be meaningful. Analysis of them will generate helpful marketing insights
- Each metric should have a benchmark aligned with my business stage and niche. Return the number, don't describe it.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
- My business: [INSERT SHORT DESCRIPTION ABOUT YOUR BUSINESS, PRODUCT/SERVICE]
- My acquisition: [DESCRIBE HOW YOU ACQUIRE CUSTOMERS]
- My activation: [DESCRIBE HOW YOU ACTIVATE CUSTOMERS]
- My monetization: [DESCRIBE YOUR MONETIZATION STRATEGY]
- Our business stage: [DESCRIBE YOUR BUSINESS STAGE]

#RESPONSE STRUCTURE:
Return a table with 4 columns:
- Metric type
- Marketing metric
- Impact on marketing
- Industry benchmark

❓ How To Use The Prompt:

  • Describe your business, how you acquire and activate customers, your monetization strategy, and your current business stage.
  • Example: "My business is an e-commerce platform specializing in eco-friendly home products. We acquire customers primarily through social media marketing, activate them through email marketing campaigns, and monetize through direct online sales. We are in the growth stage of our business."

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:

  • My business: God of Prompt, the biggest collection of easy-to-follow AI resources, AI Prompts & How-to Guides for busy Small Business Owners.
  • My acquisition: Organic traffic via SEO, social media, and email marketing campaigns.
  • My activation: Free email course, webinars, and trial access to selected resources.
  • My monetization: One-time purchase for individual and unlimited strategies; also subscriptions.
  • Our business stage: Growing with stable traction and increasing conversions.

  • Continuous Review: Regularly reassess your metrics to ensure they remain relevant as your business evolves.
  • Action-Oriented: Use the insights from these metrics to inform and adjust your marketing strategies.
  • Contextual Understanding: Understand how each metric relates to the specific nuances of your industry and market trends.
