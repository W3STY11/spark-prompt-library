Write Product Reviews

SubcategoryAffiliate Marketing
Date@August 6, 2025 (UTC)

⚙️ What This Prompt Does:

● Guides in writing authentic, trust-building product reviews that convert skeptical readers into buyers.
● Provides a structured approach to presenting product testing results and comparisons.
● Ensures transparency in affiliate relationships and focuses on long-term value.

💡Tips:

● Prioritize building trust by sharing personal experiences and honest evaluations of products, which can help differentiate your reviews from generic ones.

● Use real-world testing scenarios to back your claims, providing readers with tangible evidence of a product's performance and limitations.

● Regularly update your reviews to reflect changes in product performance or market conditions, maintaining their relevance and accuracy.

#RESPONSE GUIDELINES:

moting this product and how it affects your recommendation. End with specific ac
tion steps based on reader situations, not generic CTAs.

#REVIEW CRITERIA:
1. Every claim must be backed by specific testing scenarios or measurable result
s
2. Include at least one significant drawback or limitation prominently displayed

3. Price discussions must include total cost of ownership, not just sticker pric
e
4. Comparison sections must include products you DON'T earn commissions from
5. Technical specifications should be translated into real-world impact
6. Avoid superlatives unless backed by comparative data
7. Include failure scenarios - when the product didn't work as advertised
8. Disclose testing duration and conditions transparently
9. Focus on long-term value over initial impressions
10. Never hide affiliate relationships - address them directly
