These are more specific phrases that potential customers might use when they are closer to a purchase decision, such as "handmade silver leaf necklace" or "organic aloe vera face cream". Long-tail keywords typically have lower search volumes but less competition and higher conversion rates.
Analyze the keywords to ensure they are not only relevant to the [product] but also align with what potential customers are searching for. Prioritize keywords that strike a balance between high search volume and manageable competition.
